About DaveField

DaveField is an open research project exploring the hidden symmetry of primes. By combining mathematics, lattice geometry, and visualisation, the project seeks to reveal patterns in number theory that connect to physics, crystals, and beyond.

This site documents the journey — from conjectures and experiments, to interactive tools and visualisations. Everything here is open and shared for anyone to explore.
