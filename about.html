About DaveField

Founded by Dr Michael Maughan in 2024. DaveField is an open research project exploring entropy, information theory probing physics.

This site is new and under construction.

The Davefield project is non-commercial and as such is not funded (donations welcome - please see our Contribute Page!) and updates will be made as and when possible so please excuse our site!

In studying the way information moves through numbers we have seen glimpses of the hidden symmetry within the primes.

By combining mathematics, lattice geometry, and visualisation, the project seeks to reveal patterns in number theory that connect to physics, crystals, and beyond.

This site documents the journey — from conjectures and experiments, to interactive tools and visualisations. Everything here is open and shared for anyone to explore.
